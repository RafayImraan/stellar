◈ ZK Remittance

Private Compliance for Cross-Border Remittance

Prove you're compliant for remittance — without revealing your identity, exact amount, or location. Verified on Stellar Soroban via zero-knowledge proofs.

Submit ZK Proof & Verify On-Chain

Private fields stay in your browser. Only the ZK proof is submitted to Stellar.

How It Works

1
You hold private data

Your wallet hash, transfer amount, and country never leave your device in plaintext.

2
ZK circuit proves compliance

A Noir circuit proves: not on sanctions list, amount within legal limits, jurisdiction allowed — all at once.

3
Stellar verifies the proof

The Soroban smart contract verifies the UltraHonk proof and records a nullifier to prevent replay.
